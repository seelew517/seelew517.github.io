机器学习第一次作业

设总体均值为$\mu$，总体方差为$\sigma^2$。对上式两边求期望，得：
$$
\begin{aligned}
E(\sigma_{ML}^2) &= E\left(\frac{1}{N} \sum_{n=1}^N (x_n - \mu_{ML})^2\right)\
&= \frac{1}{N} \sum_{n=1}^N E\left((x_n - \mu_{ML})^2\right)\
&= \frac{1}{N} \sum_{n=1}^N E\left((x_n - \mu + \mu -\mu_{ML})^2\right)\
&= \frac{1}{N} \sum_{n=1}^N E\left((x_n - \mu)^2 + 2(x_n - \mu)(\mu - \mu_{ML}) + (\mu - \mu_{ML})^2\right)\
&= \frac{1}{N} \sum_{n=1}^N E\left((x_n - \mu)^2\right) + 2E\left((x_n - \mu)(\mu - \mu_{ML})\right) + E\left((\mu - \mu_{ML})^2\right)\
&= \frac{N-1}{N}\sigma^2
\end{aligned}
$$

从而得：
$$
E(\sigma_{ML}^2) - \sigma^2 = -\frac{\sigma^2}{N}
$$

因此最大似然估计的方差是有偏的(biased)。

假设$p_k \geq 0$，构造拉格朗日函数：
$$
L(p_1, p_2, \dots, p_m, \lambda) = -\sum_{k=1}^m p_k \log p_k + \lambda (\sum_{k=1}^m p_k - 1)
$$
令$\left{ \begin{matrix}\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial L}{\partial p_k} &= -\log p_k - 1 + \lambda = 0, \quad k = 1, 2, \dots, m \
\frac{\partial L}{\partial \lambda} &= \sum_{k=1}^m p_k - 1 = 0
\end{aligned}\end{matrix} \right.$

有$p_k = e^{\lambda - 1}, \quad k = 1, 2, \dots, m
$

代入第二个方程，有：
$$
\begin{aligned}
\sum_{k=1}^m p_k &= me^{\lambda - 1} = 1 \
\Rightarrow \lambda &= 1 -\ln m\
p_k &= \frac{1}{m} \textgreater 0, \quad k = 1, 2, \dots, m
\end{aligned}
$$
计算拉格朗日函数的黑塞矩阵，有：
$$
H(L) &=
\left[
\begin{matrix}
-\frac {1}{p_1} & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 1 \
0 & -\frac {1}{p_2} & \cdots & 0 & 1 \
\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \
0 & 0 & \cdots & -\frac {1}{p_m} & 1 \
1 & 1 & \cdots & 1 & 0 \
\end{matrix} \right]\
&=
\left[
\begin{matrix}
-m & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 1 \
0 & -m & \cdots & 0 & 1 \
\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \
0 & 0 & \cdots & -m & 1 \
1 & 1 & \cdots & 1 & 0 \
\end{matrix} \right]
$$
奇数阶顺序主子式均小于零，矩阵负定，所以得到的是局部极大值，也是全局最大的熵。

目标函数是严格凸的，约束集是凸集，所以最优解唯一。

设微分熵为：
$$
H(X) = -\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}p(x)\log p(x)dx
$$
根据已知条件，有：
$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}xp(x)dx=\alpha\
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}(x-\alpha)^2p(x)dx=\beta
$$
设拉格朗日函数为：
$$
L(p)=H(X)+\lambda_0\left(\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}p(x)dx-1\right)+\lambda_1\left(\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}xp(x)dx-\alpha\right)+\lambda_2\left(\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}(x-\alpha)^2p(x)dx-\beta\right)
$$
令$\frac{\partial}{\partial p(x)}L(p)=-\log p(x)-1+\lambda_0+\lambda_1x+\lambda_2(x-\alpha)^2=0
$

解得：
$$
p(x)=e^{-1+\lambda_0}\cdot e^{\lambda_1x+\lambda_2(x-\alpha)^2}\
e^{-1+\lambda_0}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}e^{\lambda_1x+\lambda_2(x-\alpha)^2}dx=1\
e^{-1+\lambda_0}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}xe^{\lambda_1x+\lambda_2(x-\alpha)^2}dx=\alpha\
e^{-1+\lambda_0}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}(x-\alpha)^2e^{\lambda_1x+\lambda_2(x-\alpha)^2}dx=\beta
$$
令$\lambda_1 = 0$，解得：
$$
\begin{aligned}
\lambda_0&=1+\log|\lambda_2|-\log \pi\
\alpha&=-\frac{1}{4\lambda_2}\
\beta&=\frac{3}{4|\lambda_2|}-\frac{1}{16\lambda_2^2}\
p(x)&=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\beta}}e^{-\frac{(x-\alpha)^2}{2\beta}}
\end{aligned}
$$
即解得的概率密度函数是正态分布。
